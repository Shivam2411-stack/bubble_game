Instructions

Welcome to the Bubble Game! Your objective is to score as many points as possible by clicking on bubbles. Each bubble you click earns you 10 points. You have 60 seconds to rack up the highest score possible, so be quick and accurate!
A 60-second countdown will begin when the game starts, and you can track your score and remaining time at the top of the screen. For every bubble hit, your score increases by 10 points. Precision and speed will be essential in achieving the highest score before time runs out.
Once the timer hits zero, the game will end, and your final score will be displayed. Good luck, and aim for the top score!
